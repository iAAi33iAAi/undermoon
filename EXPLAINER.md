# Frontier Explainer  Undermoon

## The Problem

Civilization-scale coordination is fragmented. Identity, governance, shelter, economy,
safety, and continuity are designed in silos  each with its own stack, its own politics,
its own failure modes. No single framework connects them. The people who need integrated
infrastructure most  those building at the frontier  are forced to reinvent the
substrate every time.

## The Solution

Undermoon is a modular coordination substrate that unifies six foundational domains into
one deployable architecture. It was built by a solo origin-architect, peer-registered
as the Six Domain Optimization Framework, and published for collaborative stewardship.

## Six-Domain Architecture

- **Identity & Lineage**  Sovereign identity and authorship verification. You are
-   recognized, and your work is traceable.
-   - **Governance & Consent**  Decision protocols that require consent, not hierarchy.
    -   Power is distributed by design.
    -   - **Shelter (Forever Domes)**  Infrastructure so that every person can embody a
        -   forever dome home. Physical and digital.
        -   - **Economy & Vesting**  Ethical compensation and transparent value-flow. No
            -   extraction without reciprocity.
            -   - **Safety & Kernel**  Safeguard systems (AETHEL GRID, ALUXERAC, ALPHA ALLU) that
                -   protect the substrate from capture and decay.
                -   - **Continuity & Archive**  Modular documentation and spiral archival. What is built
                    -   survives the builder.
