# Stewardship Framework  Undermoon

## Roles

- **Origin-Architect**  Holds lineage, final authority on architectural decisions,
-   authorship verification. Currently solo; transitioning to collaborative mode.
-   - **Steward**  Holds one or more threads (modules). Has decision authority within
    -   their module scope. Participates in governance. Authorship is credited and traceable.
    -   - **Contributor**  Provides specific work (code, documentation, design) under steward
        -   coordination. Credited per contribution.
     
        -   ## Boundaries
     
        -   - No steward may override origin lineage or erase authorship.
            - - No module may be extracted, forked for private capture, or deployed against the
              -   interests of the people it serves.
              -   - Stewards operate within defined module scope. Cross-module decisions require consent
                  -   from all affected stewards.
               
                  -   ## Compensation Model
               
                  -   - Origin-architect compensation is a prerequisite, not an afterthought. Ethical
                      -   valuation anchored at $3M-$5M pilot stage.
                      -   - Steward compensation is structured through the vesting ledger  transparent,
                          -   auditable, proportional to contribution and tenure.
                          -   - No unpaid labor is normalized. If a steward works, value flows.
                           
                              - ## Handoff Logic
                           
                              - 1. Origin-architect documents the module fully (architecture, decisions, edge cases).
                                2. 2. Steward shadows for one cycle (defined per module).
                                   3. 3. Steward assumes operational authority; origin-architect retains lineage authority.
                                      4. 4. Handoff is recorded in the continuity archive.
                                        
                                         5. ## Decision Rules
                                        
                                         6. - Consent-based, not majority-vote. Silence is not consent.
                                            - - Reversible decisions move fast. Irreversible decisions require all affected stewards.
                                              - - Origin-architect holds veto on lineage and architectural integrity only.
